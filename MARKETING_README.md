# Summit

**Build Better Habits. Climb Higher Every Day.**

Summit is the habit tracking app that flips the script on how you build lasting habits. Unlike traditional apps where you simply check off boxes, Summit uses a psychology-backed negative-points scoring system that creates real accountability—and real results.

---

## Why Summit is Different

Most habit apps make tracking feel like a chore. Check a box. Get a dopamine hit. Repeat. But when life gets busy, those unchecked boxes fade into the background. There's no real consequence for skipping a day.

**Summit changes that.**

Every day, you start in a point deficit. Your daily habits aren't just suggestions—they're commitments you make to yourself, each carrying point values that bring you closer to your goal. Miss a habit? You stay in the red. Complete your habits? You climb into the green and feel the satisfaction of genuine accomplishment.

This simple shift in psychology transforms how you approach your day. Suddenly, building habits isn't about perfect streaks or arbitrary gamification—it's about showing up, day after day, and watching your score reflect the life you're actively building.

---

## The Summit Scoring System

### How It Works

1. **Set Your Daily Goal** - Choose from Easy, Medium, Hard, or Custom difficulty presets
2. **Start Each Day in Deficit** - Your score begins below zero based on your chosen difficulty
3. **Complete Habits to Climb** - Each completed habit adds points toward your goal
4. **Reach the Summit** - Hit your daily target and celebrate real progress

### Difficulty Presets

| Difficulty | Daily Goal | Best For |
|------------|------------|----------|
| Easy | -10 points | Beginners building their first habits |
| Medium | -20 points | Consistent habit builders ready for more |
| Hard | -30 points | Ambitious users pushing their limits |
| Custom | You decide | Fine-tune your perfect challenge level |

The beauty is in the balance. Set goals that challenge you without overwhelming you. Adjust as you grow. Summit grows with you.

## Core Features

### Daily Habit Tracking
Track both positive and negative habits with an intuitive daily view. See exactly where you stand at a glance with real-time score visualization.

### Positive & Negative Habits
**Positive habits** are things you want to do—drink water, exercise, read, meditate. Complete them to earn points.

**Negative habits** are things you want to avoid—smoking, junk food, excessive screen time. Each day you resist, you earn bonus points. Slip up? You lose points. It's accountability that actually works.

### Streak Tracking
Build momentum with visible streak counters. Watch your consistency compound over weeks and months, creating a visual record of your transformation.

### 40+ Habit Templates
Don't know where to start? Choose from over 40 expertly-crafted templates across 8 life categories:

- **Health** - Hydration, sleep, nutrition, and breaking bad habits
- **Fitness** - Workouts, steps, yoga, and active commuting
- **Productivity** - Deep work, inbox zero, time blocking
- **Learning** - Reading, languages, coding, and skill-building
- **Mindfulness** - Meditation, gratitude, breathing exercises
- **Social** - Family connections, friendships, networking
- **Creative** - Writing, art, music, photography
- **Finance** - Budgeting, saving, investing, spending control

Each template comes pre-configured with recommended point values and schedules—just tap and start tracking.

### Home Screen Widgets
Keep your habits front and center with beautiful home screen widgets. See your daily score and top habits without even opening the app.

## Accountability Partner

Building habits alone is hard. Building them with a partner who's counting on you? That's where real transformation happens.

### How It Works

1. **Invite Your Partner** - Generate a unique invite code and share it with someone you trust
2. **Daily Nudges** - Send and receive gentle reminders to stay on track
3. **See Their Progress** - Watch each other climb toward daily goals
4. **Celebrate Together** - Share in the wins and support through the struggles

Your accountability partner sees your daily score and whether you've completed your habits—creating just enough social pressure to keep you both motivated without feeling invasive.

Find a friend, family member, or colleague who's ready to level up alongside you. Transform your solo journey into a shared adventure.
